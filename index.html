Interactive Presentation 101: How to Create An Interactive Deck

When you organize your presentation, you would like it to be attractive. Whether you display them in real-time or embed them on your website, you wish to entertain your audience. The simplest way is to form an interactive presentation. This may keep the audience's attention and even engage them.

November 3, 2022

How you are going to do this is going to depend on the scale of your audience; If you're presenting in a group or seminar, you'll be able to easily move around the room and let everyone discuss with you.

If you're speaking before a crowd or in an exceedingly large meeting with a bigger audience, it makes more sense to easily introduce your audience to a neighbor or two before entering the discussion.

You'll ask the audience to answer questions aloud or work along with your neighbors to arrange some questions about your topic or a listing of things they need to find out or create a fun icebreaker game. Search for what kinds of icebreaker questions may make sense for the context of your presentation.
